How to Play

1. Roll the dice to get a group of letters. Dice 1 = worst letters, 5 = best letters, 6 = choose freely.

2. Click letters to buy them, cost shown in coins (like Scrabble letter values).

3. Drag bought letters into the word builder to form words.

4. Check words to gain score. Used letters are removed from your bank unless dragged back.

5. You can create multiple words each roll.

6. Finish the game to export your words and scores as CSV.
