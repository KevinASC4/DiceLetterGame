How to Play

Objective: Buy letter tiles, form words, and sell them for profit.

  • Start with $200 in play money.
  • There are 108 letter tiles and 9 wild tiles (*).
  • Roll the dice to draw a number of tiles (2–6).
  • Decide to buy all drawn tiles or discard them. Price = sum of pips squared.
  • You can only hold up to 8 tiles (not counting wild tiles).
  • Form words using your tiles. One wild tile can substitute for any letter.
  • Sell words to the bank for money = sum of pips squared.
  • Keep track of your actions for CSV/Excel export.
  • Finish the game to export a full log of rolls, purchases, discards, and word sales.
